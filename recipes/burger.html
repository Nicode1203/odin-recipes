Perfect Homemade Burger

Satisfy your cravings with our Perfect Homemade Burger recipe. This juicy and flavorful burger is made with freshly ground beef, seasoned to perfection, and grilled to your desired level of doneness. Topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and your favorite condiments, this burger is perfect for any occasion and will surely be a hit at your next cookout or family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix gently until just combined.
  2. Divide the beef mixture into four equal portions and shape each portion into a patty, about 3/4-inch thick. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty with your thumb to prevent them from puffing up while cooking.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a stovetop, heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Place the burger patties on the preheated grill or pan. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty to melt. You can also toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Remove the burger patties from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on the bottom half of a toasted bun. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, onions, pickles, and your favorite condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  8. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately. Enjoy your Perfect Homemade Burger!
